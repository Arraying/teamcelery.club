Who We Are

More than a team of marshland apiaceae

TeamCelery's roots were founded on the 23rd of November 2018 as the guild 'Celery' on the Hypixel Minecraft server focusing on BedWars. The original roster featured Arraying, Caked_, yoonkles, Zizuz101 and 0_Epic_Pvp_0.

On the 5th of October 2019 the CS:GO team that still stands today was founded. Whilst times have certainly changed, the core of the team has not. All founding members of the organization are still active players on the roster. Early members pants and Nelectric are no longer part of CS scene. Currently, TeamCelery is competing in the EU region under the TeamCelery tag.

Join Us

Become part vegetable

You are welcome to casually play with TeamCelery. Often, we will invite friendly teammates to join us in our Steam group. Alternatively, you can request to join, however this method is not often successful.

Momentarily, there are no opportunities to become apart of the main roster. If opportunity should present itself in the future, being a part of the Steam group is the first step. Regardless of if you are in the main roster or not: you're family.
